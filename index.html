How to Use This Tool

The purpose of this tool is to generate story ideas! The generated story will be split into its respective plot points. The method of generation used is POCL (Partial Order Causal Link), where a story goal is generated, and then the story works backwards to make a logical story out of it.

This generator can also be used as a fantasy name generator! A fantasy name generator is built into the fantasy genre for cohesiveness. I recommend putting Genre Intensity up to 1.0 if this is your goal.

Descriptiveness

This value determines the likelihood of adjectives and adverbs appearing throughout the story (while still following grammar rules).

Genre

Genre will significantly change the tone of the story by restricting word choice to genre-specific language. Story outcomes are also affected by this parameter, which changes the plot points it can generate.

Genre Intensity

This value affects how strictly the story draws from the genre-specific word pool. Values less than 1.0 (the maximum value) will sometimes fall back to the neutral language set.
